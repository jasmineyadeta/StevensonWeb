About

Mike Stevenson

Mike Stevenson is a seasoned professional with over forty (40) years’ experience in the engineering and construction industry. He has participated in major infrastructure projects in North America, Europe and Africa. Mike’s construction experience includes power, mining, building, rail and infrastructure projects, where he has held supervisory positions in business operations, procurement, project controls and construction. He also holds a Project Management professional designation from the Project Management Institute (PMI). In the last five (5) years, Mike has managed a program of utility projects with a team of up to 24 project managers, construction managers and project controls personnel reporting to him. He has worked on multiple design build projects since 1985 both during the proposal and in the execution phases giving him a sound understanding of engineering and working practices.
